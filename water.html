Water Crunch

Structural Demand

  • Population growth and urbanisation in water-stressed regions.
  • Industrial pull: semiconductors, data centres, battery and critical-materials refining.
  • Energy transition: cooling loads, hydropower variability, hydrogen/e-fuels water needs.

Supply & Quality Constraints

  • Climate variability, earlier snowmelt, and declining seasonal reliability.
  • Groundwater overdraft and slow aquifer recharge.
  • Ageing pipes and contaminants (e.g., PFAS) raising treatment costs.

Pricing, Policy & Capex

  • Tariff reforms and drought surcharges (cost-reflective pricing).
  • Massive capex into reuse, desalination, membranes, PFAS removal, AMI, and leakage control.
  • Allocation/rights tension in stressed basins; ag-to-urban transfers.
